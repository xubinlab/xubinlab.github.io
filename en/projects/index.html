PROJECTS

Representative project cases will be gradually added here. Each case follows the structure: "Problem Background → Goals → Technical Solution → Results → Reflection". Currently only structural examples are provided for future content.

Example: Aircraft Engine Full-Flowpath Simulation Platform (Hardware-Software Co-Design)

  • Problem Background: Aerodynamic and thermal characteristics evaluation of multi-stage fans, compressors, combustors, and turbines, with high requirements for both accuracy and computation time.
  • Goals: Obtain engineering-acceptable accuracy results within given computing power and time budgets, and achieve significant and reproducible acceleration on custom chips.
  • My Role: Solver design and implementation, implicit format and sparse matrix solver optimization, hardware-software co-design discussion and implementation, testing and benchmarking.
  • Technical Highlights: Equation non-dimensionalization, RANS/LES model selection, Jacobian construction, Krylov solvers and preconditioning, MPI parallelization, chip-side matrix operator deployment.
  • Results (Illustrative): On several internal test cases, compared with commercial software, main engineering indicators showed deviations within engineering-acceptable ranges; under the same core count, significant acceleration was achieved for some cases (specific data will be placed on dedicated pages).
